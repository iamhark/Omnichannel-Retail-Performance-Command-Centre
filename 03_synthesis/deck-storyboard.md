# Power BI Page Storyboard

This is a dashboard storyboard, not a slide deck.

| Page | Decision question | Required visuals | Evidence |
|---|---|---|---|
| Executive performance | Where is commercial performance changing, and which exception needs attention? | KPI cards, revenue/contribution trend, channel mix, exception matrix | CLM-001, CLM-006 |
| Customer and retention | Which customer groups require protection or reactivation? | RFM segment bars, retention trend, CLV scatter, risk table | CLM-002, CLM-003 |
| Product and store operations | Which same-format locations and categories require diagnosis? | Store variance bars, category matrix, product margin scatter, simulated-store map | CLM-004 |
| Marketing and channel efficiency | Which simulated channels earn or destroy attributed margin? | Funnel, ROI/ROAS bars, spend-margin scatter, campaign trend | CLM-005 |

Every page must display: `Business-case data: UCI Online Retail public transactions plus deterministic simulated omnichannel extensions.`
